Payments to the CRA

Call if you are unable to pay in full or on time

We understand that people and businesses sometimes find themselves in circumstances where they are unable to pay in full and on time.

By giving us a call, you can let us know you need more time and we can help you:

  • Defer a repayment to a later date
  • Start or change a payment arrangement
  • Find out how to minimize interest and penalties
  • Discuss other ways to reduce or resolve your debt

Set up a payment arrangement

If you want to set up a payment arrangement yourself online or with an automated phone service, refer to: Arrange to pay your personal debt over time.

Before you call

Make sure you have the following when you call to answer our questions to validate your identity:

  • Social Insurance Number (or business number)
  • Full name and date of birth (or business name)
  • Your complete address (or complete business address)
  • Your personal (or business) account open or an assessed return, notice of assessment, reassessment, or other tax document
