Types of debt the CRA collects

The CRA collects amounts due to the CRA and some amounts due to other areas of the Government of Canada.

Amounts from the CRA

The CRA collects amounts due for:

  • Personal income tax
  • Corporate income tax
  • Payroll deductions
  • GST/HST
  • Tax credits and benefits for individuals such as GST/HST credit and Canada child benefit (CCB)

We also collect overpayments of COVID-19 benefits and subsidies:

  • Repaying COVID-19 benefits for individuals
  • Repaying COVID-19 subsidies for businesses
