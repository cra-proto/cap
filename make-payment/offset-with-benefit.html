Offset your debt with a benefit: Payments to the CRA

How to pay the amount you owe

The payment and debt must be both to your name or business. We cannot transfer payments between:

  • different individuals
  • personal and business accounts

How to offset a debt

To put in a request to offset a debt, you can call us.

If you received Form x aka collections

We will automatically apply certain payments to an outstanding debt if you are already in collections procedures. Call us to learn about specific cases where we don't automatically offset a debt, such as the Canada Child Benefit.

Processing time

  • Considered as paid: same day you made it (stamped by the bank employee)
  • Updated in your CRA account: up to 2 business days after we receive the payment. You can confirm if we received a payment

If your payment is still not showing after 2 business days(shouldn't we include after how long?), you can resolve your payment issue
