Pay with a debit card through CRA's Pay Simply service: Payments to the CRA

You can make payments directly to the CRA using your bank access card (Interac Debit, Visa Debit, or Debit Mastercard) through CRA's secure electronic service called My Payment.

On this page

  • What you can and can't pay
  • How to pay the amount you owe
  • How long the payment takes to be received and processed

What you can and can't pay

Payments we accept online through My Payment
Indivduals
  • Income tax (T1) amount owing
  • Income tax (T1) instalment payments
  • Canada Child Benefit (CCB) repayment/overpayment
  • Canada Dental Benefit repayment/overpayment
  • COVID-19 benefit repayment/overpayment (CERB, etc.)
  • GST/HST credit repayment
  • One time top-up to Canada Housing Benefit repayment/overpayment
  • Alberta credits repayment/overpayment
  • Ontario credits repayment/overpayment
  • Part XIII withholding tax (non-resident)
  • Universal Child Care Benefit repayment/overpayment
Businesses
  • Corporation income tax (T2)
  • Corporation income tax (T2) instalment payments
  • GST/HST remittances
  • Source deductions (payroll)
  • Air Travellers Security Charge
  • COVID-19 business subsidy overpayments (CEWS, etc.) except rent subisdy
  • Excise duties
  • Excise tax (various things)
  • Excise tax on insurance premiums
  • Fuel charge
  • Luxury tax
  • Nova Scotia Workers' Compensation Board payments
  • Part XIII withholding tax (non-resident)
  • Softwood lumber products export charge
  • Underused housing tax
  • Information returns penalty
  • Rent subisdies repayment
  • Workplace Safety and Insurance Board (Ontario)
Payments we do not accept online through My Payment
Individuals
  • Advanced Canada workers benefit repayment/overpayment
  • Climate Action Incentive (CAI) repayment
  • Individual income tax instalment payments - No Calc. option
  • TFSA amoung owing
  • Security deposit (Part IX) incl. GST/HST
Businesses
  • Various provincial/territorial benefit/credit overpayments
  • Garnishment on behalf of an employee
  • Information Return (RZ), T5, RRSP, TFSA, etc. penalty
  • Part XIII.1 tax on foreign banks
  • Part XIV additional tax on corporation
  • Security deposit (Part IX) incl. GST/HST
Trusts
  • Trust income tax (T3) (available February 2024)
Charities
  • Re-register a charity
  • Charities penalties

    Pay now using My Payment

    Hours of service

    21 hours a day, seven days a week.

    Have a secure connection

    Refer to: Access online services safely

    Use a modern broswer
    You will not be able to access My Payment using an older browser that does not support the latest security standards. Refer to: Your browser and troubleshooting tips

How long the payment takes to be received and processed

  • Considered as paid: the same or next business day
  • Updated in your CRA account: up to 3 business days after the payment is received. You can confirm if a payment was received.

If your payment is still not showing after 3 business days, resolve a payment issue.
