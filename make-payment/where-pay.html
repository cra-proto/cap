Note: The CRA does not accept the following forms of payments:

  • Cryptocurrency
    We do not accept any form of cryptocurrency (e.g. Bitcoin) as payment
  • Gift cards
    We do not accept any gift cards as a form of payment
  • Cash by mail
    Cash can only be used at any Canada post retail location

Steps on how to avoid CRA scams

You can also review the Method to pay.

Processing time

  • Considered as paid: the same day it was paid (stamped by the clerk)
  • Updated in your CRA account: up to 2 business days after we receive the payment. You can confirm if we received a payment.

If your payment is still not showing after 2 business days, you can resolve a payment issue.
