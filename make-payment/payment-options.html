Processing time

  • Considered as paid: the same day you made the payment (stamped by the clerk)
  • Updated in your CRA account: up to 2 business days after we receive the payment. You can confirm if we received your payment.

If your payment is still not showing after 2 business days, you can resolve a payment issue.
